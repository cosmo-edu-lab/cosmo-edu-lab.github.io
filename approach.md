Cosmo-Edu-Lab is built on three commitments:

### 1. Integrated into the school curriculum

Materials are written for 15–19 year olds with a standard physics or science background and elementary formalism. Astronomical and cosmological objects, length scales, observational instruments, and historical context are introduced through interactive, gamified activities.

### 2. An inquiry-based approach

We aim to engage students in the process of scientific discovery, encouraging them to ask questions, formulate hypotheses, and draw conclusions from evidence.

### 3. Real observational data on a digital platform

Activities use real astronomical datasets, from solar system objects to galaxies to large-scale galactic networks. Students are guided to analyse data according to their curriculum knowledge and extract quantitative predictions. Activities are integrated into an all-in-one digital platform that lets students explore the universe in a hands-on way.
